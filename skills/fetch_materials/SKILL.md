\# Fetch Study Materials Skill



\## Description

This skill fetches relevant study materials (books, articles, videos, tutorials) for any subject and topic.



\## Input Required

\- \*\*Subject\*\*: Math, Science, History, Languages, Coding

\- \*\*Topic\*\*: Specific topic within the subject

\- \*\*Level\*\*: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced



\## Output Provided

\- List of recommended study materials

\- Resource links and descriptions

\- Difficulty ratings

\- Estimated study time



\## How It Works

1\. User inputs subject and topic

2\. Skill searches for relevant resources using Google Gemini AI

3\. Filters and curates best materials

4\. Returns organized list with links

5\. Saves results to data/materials.json

\*\*Example 4\*\*: Subject: Computer Networks
