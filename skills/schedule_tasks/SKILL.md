\# Schedule Study Tasks Skill



\## Description

This skill creates organized study schedules based on your subjects, topics, and available time. It breaks down large topics into manageable tasks with deadlines.



\## Input Required

\- \*\*Subject\*\*: The subject to study (DSA, OS, Python, CN, etc.)

\- \*\*Topic\*\*: Specific topic to learn

\- \*\*Available Time\*\*: Hours per week you can study

\- \*\*Deadline\*\*: When you need to complete (days from now)

\- \*\*Priority\*\*: High, Medium, or Low



\## Output Provided

\- Weekly study schedule with time blocks

\- Daily task breakdown

\- Estimated completion date

\- Progress milestones

\- Reminder notifications

\- Saved to data/schedules.json



\## How It Works

1\. User inputs subject, topic, and timeline

2\. Skill calculates optimal study duration

3\. Creates day-wise schedule with time blocks

4\. Sets realistic deadlines and milestones

5\. Generates reminder alerts

6\. Stores schedule in JSON file
